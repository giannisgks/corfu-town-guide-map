Spianada Square

The Spianada is a large square in the city of Corfu, Greece. It is the largest square in Greece and is located in front of the Old Fortress of the city of Corfu.
The Spianada is divided into two sections: the Upper Square (Pano Plateia) and the Lower Square (Kato Plateia). The Upper Square is adorned with architectural gems like the Maitland Monument, the Venetian fountain, the Music Pavilion or "Palko" and the monument commemorating the Union of the Ionian Islands with Greece. The Lower Square is dominated by the cricket pitch.

Old Fortress

The Old Fortress of Corfu is a Venetian-built fortification located on the eastern side of the Corfu town. This iconic structure played a crucial role in the defense of the island, and its impressive architecture showcases the Venetian influence in Corfu.
The fortress includes several notable points of interest such as St. George's Church, which resembles an ancient Doric temple, and the lighthouse at the top, which offers stunning views of Corfu and the Ionian Sea.
